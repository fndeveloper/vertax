Vulnerability Assessment (VA) and Penetration Testing (PT) are two distinct but related cybersecurity practices used to identify and assess security weaknesses in systems and applications. VA uses automated tools to scan for vulnerabilities, while PT involves manually attempting to exploit those vulnerabilities in a controlled environment to simulate real-world attacks.
