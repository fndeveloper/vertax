Engineered for 24/7 functionality in even the harshest environments, our highly scalable solution enhances terminal efficiency and can save businesses millions. buoySPOT TM

Our intelligent AI-based solution automatically detects and classifies various types of container damages — including dents, bends, rust, corrosion, and more. It precisely measures and compares the damage to ensure accurate assessments.

By significantly reducing false damage claims, the system enhances safety for both vessel crews and ground personnel. It helps terminal operators and shipping companies save millions by increasing operational capacity, boosting productivity, and minimizing delays.

buoySPY TM

Our cutting-edge AI-based solution enables round-the-clock monitoring of terminal areas or any designated premises, operating reliably in all weather conditions and seasons.

It automatically detects and alerts for safety violations such as individuals without helmets, safety vests, masks, gloves, and other protective gear. The system also identifies fire or smoke incidents, stray animals, littering, and can monitor and count vehicles in real time within any area or facility.

By providing instant alerts and enabling swift action, the solution helps reduce safety breaches, prevent losses, and maintain a secure environment.

Developed using advanced AI, deep learning, and machine learning technologies, this solution stands at the forefront of modern surveillance and safety systems. It is highly in demand for its effectiveness in enhancing both operational safety and security.

callMatics TM

"callMatics" is an advanced AI-driven call center solution that enables human-like conversations without the need for human agents. It allows both customers and employees to interact naturally with the system via voice, retrieving or sharing information using everyday language. The system connects over standard telephone lines, accesses corporate databases, and resolves queries efficiently and intelligently.

Our solution can automate various services, including customer support, IT help desk assistance, marketing outreach, market research, grocery ordering, and more.

callMatics features unlimited virtual agents, supports integration with countless data sources, operates 24/7, handles thousands of calls simultaneously, and offers multilingual support in over 100 languages.

With the ability to automate up to 70% of your call center operations, callMatics delivers significant cost savings while improving efficiency and productivity. Built using deep learning and machine learning techniques, it stands as a proven and powerful AI-based innovation.

planetSCAN TM

PlanetScan is a next-generation solution that leverages Artificial Intelligence and cutting-edge GIS technologies to analyze satellite imagery and extract detailed demographic, geographic, and social insights for any selected location worldwide.

With the power to count and classify objects and settlement types—such as apartments, villas, slums, industrial areas, and planned residential zones—PlanetScan also offers advanced analysis of agricultural land, crop types, land usage, parks, water bodies, and sports facilities.

This intelligent platform enables businesses, town planners, infrastructure developers, and real estate experts to optimize location strategies, identify high-potential areas, and make data-driven decisions. From franchise placement to utility planning, PlanetScan offers unmatched accuracy and operational efficiency.

Built on deep learning and machine learning algorithms, the system supports multi-layered data uploads, comparative spatial analytics, and region-specific insights including population trends, gender distribution, housing density, market zones, entertainment hubs, and more. What traditionally takes months to analyze can now be achieved in just a few hours with PlanetScan.

Whether you're targeting the right audience or planning your next business expansion, PlanetScan helps you assess, visualize, and act with confidence.
